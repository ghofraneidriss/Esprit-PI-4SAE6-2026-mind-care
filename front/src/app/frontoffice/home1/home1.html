Clinical context

Understanding Alzheimer’s in one paragraph

Alzheimer’s disease is a progressive brain disorder that gradually affects memory, orientation, language, and behaviour. Early, consistent documentation of changes—together with stimulating routines and informed family dialogue—helps medical teams tailor support.

MindCare is a decision-support and coordination layer: it does not diagnose or prescribe, but it gives you one disciplined place to combine engagement data, movement context when used, incident notes, and community perspective—in English, with modern, accessible design.
